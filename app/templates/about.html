About NJIT Global Explorer

This project is a research initiative to determine the impact of the NJIT Study Abroad Program. Our goal is to understand and enhance the experiences of students participating in global educational opportunities.

Data Sources for Charts

  • Study Abroad Participation - Sourced from NAFSA, a non-profit organization for international education professionals.
  • Study Abroad Duration - Data provided by IIE Open Doors, a comprehensive information resource on international students and scholars.
  • Cultural Competence from Study Abroad - Information gathered from AIFS Abroad, a leading provider of study abroad programs.
  • Professional Readiness from Study Abroad - Data collected by IES Abroad, a not-for-profit provider with 140+ study abroad and internship programs.
